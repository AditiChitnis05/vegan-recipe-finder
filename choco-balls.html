Choco-Date Balls Recipe

About this recipe

These Choco-Date Energy Balls are the perfect on-the-go snack! Packed with natural sweetness from dates, healthy fats from nuts, and a rich cocoa flavor — they’re easy to make, kid-friendly, and totally satisfying.

📝 Ingredients

  • 1 cup pitted dates (soft)
  • 1/2 cup almonds or cashews
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Shredded coconut or cocoa powder (for rolling)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. In a food processor, pulse the nuts until crumbly.
  2. Add dates, cocoa powder, chia seeds, coconut oil, and salt. Blend until it forms a sticky dough.
  3. Roll mixture into small balls with your hands.
  4. Optional: Roll balls in shredded coconut or cocoa powder for coating.
  5. Chill in the fridge for 15–20 minutes before serving. Store leftovers in an airtight container.

✨ Tips & Variations

  • Use peanut butter for added flavor & stickiness.
  • Add protein powder for a post-workout version.
  • Swap nuts with seeds for a nut-free option.
